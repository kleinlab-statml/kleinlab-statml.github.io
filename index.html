Welcome to the Research Group Methods of Big Data led by Prof. Dr. Nadja Klein and located at the Scientific Computing Center, Department of Informatics, Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. In addition to teaching graduate courses on Bayesian statistics and machine learning, we are involved in several research projects.

We are welcoming applications for PhDs and Postdocs (in particular also with a focus on probabilistic learning & methods for big data). For more information, see here.
